\chapter{Kiem thu va Danh gia}

\section{Phuong phap kiem thu}

Viec kiem thu duoc thuc hien tren moi thiet bi ao trong Mininet sau khi trien khai day du NAT, ACL, Load Balancer va OSPF. Cac cong cu su dung:

\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{ping / hping3:} Kiem tra ket noi va do tro Round-Trip Time (RTT).
    \item \textbf{iperf3:} Do thong luong TCP/UDP giua cac cap may chu.
    \item \textbf{iptables -L -n -v:} Xac nhan so goi bi chan boi ACL.
    \item \textbf{load\_balancer.py:} Ghi log CSV de ve bieu do hieu nang can bang tai.
    \item \textbf{plot\_charts.py:} Xuat cac bieu do PNG phuc vu bao cao.
\end{itemize}

\section{So sanh Thong luong Truoc va Sau bao mat}

Hinh \ref{fig:throughput} so sanh thong luong TCP do duoc giua cac cap ket noi trong ba kich ban: khong bao mat, co NAT+ACL, va co them Load Balancer.

\begin{figure}[H]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=1\linewidth]{chart3_throughput_comparison.png}
    \caption{So sanh Throughput Truoc -- Sau khi bat NAT+ACL}
    \label{fig:throughput}
\end{figure}

Nhan xet:
\begin{itemize}
    \item Kich ban \textbf{Inside -> DMZ (HTTP):} Thong luong giam tu 94.2~Mbps (khong bao mat) xuong 88.4~Mbps sau NAT+ACL -- overhead chi 6.1\%.
    \item Kich ban \textbf{Inside -> Outside:} Giam tu 87.6~Mbps xuong 79.3~Mbps -- overhead 9.5\%.
    \item Kich ban \textbf{Outside -> DMZ (Static NAT):} Giam tu 89.1~Mbps xuong 81.7~Mbps -- overhead 8.3\%.
    \item Overhead NAT+ACL nho hon 10\%, chap nhan duoc cho he thong san xuat.
\end{itemize}

\section{So sanh Do tre (Latency)}

Hinh \ref{fig:latency} so sanh do tre RTT qua 30 vong do trong 3 kich ban: khong bao mat, co NAT+ACL, va co them Load Balancer.

\begin{figure}[H]
    \centering
    \includegraphics[width=1\linewidth]{chart4_latency_comparison.png}
    \caption{So sanh Latency (RTT) theo cac kich ban bao mat}
    \label{fig:latency}
\end{figure}

Nhan xet:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Khong bao mat:} RTT trung binh khoang 0.8~--~1.0~ms.
    \item \textbf{Co NAT+ACL:} RTT tang len 2.0~--~2.5~ms do qua trinh tra cuu bang NAT va kiem tra ACL.
    \item \textbf{Co them Load Balancer:} RTT khoang 2.5~--~3.0~ms, tang them mot chut do chi phi chuyen huong.
    \item Tat ca cac kich ban deu duoi 5~ms, thoa man yeu cau hieu nang da de ra.
\end{itemize}
